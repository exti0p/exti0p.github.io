About me

2022-03-01 :: exti0p
#infosec 

After a master of Engineering in Computer Science and Cybersecurity at CentraleSupélec, one of France’s top Engineering schools, I started out as a freelancer in InfoSec.

Some events drew me slowly to security in middle school and I stuck to it since then. I really like investigations, thus, I am mainly into digital forensics, which deals with reverse engineering and cryptology. I also appreciate web security and penetration testing.

I am a member of SHRECS which is our academic CTF team with fellow pupils of my engineering school. We write some writeups regarding CTF that we participated in, you can take a gander at them here.
